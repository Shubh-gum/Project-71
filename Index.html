What is new

  • Examination through Edunext

    Shemford Futuristi has become the 1st CBSE school in the Siddharthnagar district who has come up with an online examination module for the students. As a responsible school we have taken up the responsibility to deliver the best available world class education to our students even during this critical situations Adding to the above matter ,we have organised a two day Parents' Workshop (2nd &3rd July 2020) on "Steps for Online Examination Module".

  • Teacher Orientation program

    Educators are the basic pillars of the Education system. But as the pillars need proper maintenance and upgradation so do the Educators. We had organised a five day Teachers' orientation program to carter the need of the educators especially to acquaint them with the world class education system.During these days we have discussed many news things like experiential learning,project work,effective lesson planning,service rules,code of conduct and recent changes made by CBSE in the assessment pattern.
